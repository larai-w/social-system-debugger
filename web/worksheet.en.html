① Predict (hypothesis)

Which parameter (slider) will you move, and what do you think will happen to the town? Write it in your own words before you touch anything.

② Experiment (what you changed)

Record each parameter you moved and its value, "before → after".

Parameter you movedBeforeAfter

③ Observe (what happened?)

How did the banners (warning messages), numbers, and graphs change? Write what you actually saw.

④ Reflect (make meaning)

In the real world, what does this correspond to?

If you could rewind just one thing, what would you change — and why?

This app is a fictional simulation. No real place names, people, or ongoing political topics appear in it. Think of it as "a story about structures that can happen to any society" — not about anyone or anywhere in particular.
When you show your results to others, share them as an experiment (don't name and judge specific people, regions, or parties).
